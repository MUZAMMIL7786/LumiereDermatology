Hair Restoration & Treatment Services

Comprehensive solutions for all types of hair loss and scalp conditions

FUE Hair Transplant

Surgical

Follicular Unit Extraction - the gold standard for hair restoration

Procedure Details

  • Technology: Robotic-assisted ARTAS system for maximum precision and minimal trauma
  • Process: Individual hair follicles extracted from donor area (typically back of scalp), then meticulously implanted into recipient areas
  • Follicles per session: 1,000-4,000 grafts per procedure
  • Recovery time: 7-10 days for visible healing; full results in 12-18 months
  • Scarring: Minimal - small circular scars at extraction sites, virtually undetectable

Ideal Candidates

  • Male pattern baldness (Norwood Scale Stage 2-6)
  • Female pattern hair loss with sufficient donor density
  • Alopecia from trauma or burns
  • Beard and eyebrow restoration
  • Receding hairlines and crown thinning

Advantages

  • ✓ Permanent results - transplanted hair continues to grow for life
  • ✓ Natural appearance with undetectable hairline
  • ✓ Minimal scarring compared to strip methods
  • ✓ Minimal downtime - can return to work in 7-10 days
  • ✓ Multiple sessions possible without permanent damage

Post-Procedure Care

  • Days 1-7: Gentle hair washing with prescribed solutions
  • Weeks 2-4: Avoid strenuous exercise and sweating
  • Months 1-3: Hair shedding phase (normal and expected)
  • Months 3-6: New hair growth begins to emerge
  • Months 9-18: Full density and growth achieved
  • Ongoing: Monthly growth monitoring appointments

Cost & Investment

₹2,00,000 - ₹6,00,000+ depending on graft count and complexity

Includes consultation, surgical fees, post-operative care package

DHI (Direct Hair Implantation)

Surgical

Advanced technique for precision implantation without pre-created channels

What Makes DHI Different

  • Direct implantation using specialized Choi pens with minimal scalp trauma
  • No pre-made recipient sites - implants created during insertion
  • Ideal angle and depth control for natural hair growth patterns
  • Faster graft placement with higher survival rates

Advantages Over Standard FUE

  • ✓ Reduced scalp trauma and bleeding
  • ✓ Faster healing (5-7 days vs 10+ days)
  • ✓ Higher graft survival rate (95%+)
  • ✓ More natural-looking results with precise angle control
  • ✓ Minimal post-operative discomfort

Procedure Duration & Timeline

  • Extraction: 3-4 hours
  • Implantation: 2-3 hours
  • Total procedure: 6-8 hours under local anesthesia
  • Can combine with PRP therapy in same session

Cost & Investment

₹3,00,000 - ₹8,00,000+ (premium pricing due to advanced technique)

PRP Therapy for Hair Growth

Non-Surgical

Regenerative medicine using concentrated platelets to stimulate natural hair growth

How PRP Works

  • Blood drawn and processed to extract platelet-rich plasma (PRP)
  • PRP contains growth factors: PDGF, VEGF, FGF, IGF-1
  • Injected into scalp at follicle level to stimulate dormant hair
  • Promotes follicle regeneration and extends growth phase

Treatment Protocol

  • Initial phase: 3 sessions, 1 month apart
  • Maintenance: 1-2 sessions every 6 months
  • Each session: 15-30 minutes procedure + 30-min hair analysis
  • Can combine with FUE, DHI, or topical treatments

Ideal For

  • Early to mid-stage hair loss (Norwood Stage 2-4)
  • Androgenetic alopecia (pattern hair loss)
  • Telogen effluvium recovery
  • Post-transplant healing and graft strengthening
  • Alopecia areata (immune-mediated hair loss)

Expected Results Timeline

  • Week 2-4: Reduced hair shedding
  • Month 2-3: Improved hair thickness and density
  • Month 3-6: Visible new hair growth
  • Month 6+: 30-40% improvement in hair density on average

Cost & Investment

₹15,000 - ₹35,000 per session | Package: ₹40,000 - ₹90,000 (3 sessions)

Low-Level Laser Therapy (LLLT)

Non-Surgical

FDA-approved photobiomodulation to stimulate hair follicle activity

Technology & Mechanism

  • 650-1000nm wavelength lasers for deep scalp penetration
  • Stimulates ATP production in follicle cells
  • Increases blood flow to hair roots
  • Extends anagen (growth) phase of hair cycle

Treatment Protocol

  • Clinic sessions: 2-3 times per week, 15-20 minutes each
  • Or home device: 3-5 times per week (we provide FDA-approved devices)
  • Minimum duration: 3-6 months for visible results
  • Can be combined with medications, PRP, or supplements

Advantages

  • ✓ Non-invasive with zero downtime
  • ✓ FDA-cleared technology
  • ✓ No medications or side effects
  • ✓ Can be used with other treatments
  • ✓ Portable home devices available
  • ✓ Effective for all hair types

Clinical Results

  • 20-35% improvement in hair density
  • Visible hair growth in 3-4 months of consistent use
  • Works best for early-stage hair loss
  • Effective for both male and female pattern baldness

Cost & Investment

Clinic sessions: ₹8,000 - ₹15,000/month | Home device: ₹50,000 - ₹1,50,000 (one-time)

Advanced Scalp Treatments

Medical

Specialized treatments for scalp conditions, infections, and follicle health

Scalp Conditions Treated

  • Androgenetic Alopecia (AGA): Genetic pattern hair loss in men and women
  • Alopecia Areata: Autoimmune condition causing patchy hair loss
  • Telogen Effluvium: Temporary diffuse hair shedding from stress, illness, or medications
  • Scarring Alopecia: Permanent hair loss from inflammation or trauma
  • Seborrheic Dermatitis: Oily, flaking scalp condition
  • Psoriasis of Scalp: Thick, scaly patches with inflammation
  • Folliculitis: Bacterial or fungal infection of hair follicles

Diagnostic Services

  • Trichoscopy: Microscopic examination of hair shafts and follicles
  • Pull test: Assess hair shedding rate (normal: 1-3 hairs per pull)
  • Blood panel: Iron, ferritin, B12, thyroid, DHT levels
  • Scalp biopsy: When scarring alopecia suspected
  • Genetic testing: Identify genetic predisposition patterns

Treatment Modalities

  • Steroid injections: Direct injection into affected areas for alopecia areata
  • Medicated shampoos: Antifungal, antibacterial, or keratolytic formulations
  • Scalp peels: Chemical exfoliation for buildup and sebum control
  • Therapeutic massage: Increase blood flow and reduce tension
  • Personalized scalp care routine: Customized shampoo + conditioner system

Laser Hair Removal

Cosmetic

Permanent reduction of unwanted hair with advanced laser technology

How It Works

  • Laser emits concentrated light at specific wavelengths
  • Melanin in hair follicles absorbs the light energy
  • Heat destroys the follicle matrix and papilla
  • Hair is ejected from follicle over next 1-2 weeks
  • Multiple treatments needed as hair grows in cycles

Laser Systems & Skin Types

  • Candela GentleMax Pro Plus: 755nm alexandrite, best for fair to olive skin
  • Nd:YAG Laser (1064nm): Deep penetration, safe for all skin types including dark skin
  • Diode Laser (808nm): Fast, efficient, suitable for most skin types
  • IPL (Intense Pulsed Light): Broad spectrum, best for light to medium skin

Treatment Areas

  • Face: upper lip, cheeks, chin, sideburns
  • Body: arms, legs, back, chest, abdomen
  • Intimate areas: bikini line, Brazilian, full genital area
  • Other: underarms, hands, feet, shoulders

Session Protocol

  • Number of sessions: 6-8 sessions for 85-95% hair reduction
  • Spacing: 4-6 weeks apart (aligned with hair growth cycle)
  • Session duration: 15 minutes (small areas) to 60 minutes (large areas)
  • Comfort: Cooling system reduces discomfort; minimal pain sensation
  • Post-care: Avoid sun exposure for 2 weeks; use SPF 30+

Results & Expectations

  • After 1 session: 10-15% hair reduction
  • After 3 sessions: 40-50% hair reduction, visible smoothness
  • After 6 sessions: 85-90% hair reduction, very smooth skin
  • Regrowth: lighter, finer, slower than original hair
  • Maintenance: 1-2 sessions yearly to maintain results

Cost & Investment

Small area (upper lip, underarms): ₹8,000 - ₹15,000/session | Medium area (legs, arms): ₹20,000 - ₹40,000/session | Large area (full body): ₹60,000 - ₹1,00,000/session

Medical

Conditions Treated

  • Spider Veins (Telangiectasia): Small dilated facial capillaries

Psoriasis & Eczema Treatment

Medical

Comprehensive dermatological care for inflammatory skin conditions

Psoriasis Subtypes & Treatment

  • Plaque Psoriasis: Most common (85%); thick, scaly patches
  • Guttate Psoriasis: Small, teardrop-shaped lesions
  • Inverse Psoriasis: Affects skin folds; smooth, inflamed patches
  • Erythrodermic Psoriasis: Severe, widespread inflammation (medical emergency)
  • Nail Psoriasis: Pitting, discoloration, separation from nail bed

Treatment Modalities

  • Topical: Corticosteroids, calcineurin inhibitors, vitamin D analogs, retinoids
  • Systemic: Methotrexate, acitretin, cyclosporine for moderate-severe cases
  • Biologics: TNF-α inhibitors, IL-23/IL-17 inhibitors (newest, most effective)
  • Phototherapy: UVB, PUVA, excimer laser (308nm) for targeted treatment
  • Combination therapy: Multiple modalities for optimal control

Eczema (Atopic Dermatitis) Treatment

  • Topical steroids: Low to high potency depending on severity and location
  • Topical calcineurin inhibitors: Steroid-sparing alternative
  • Emollients & moisturizers: Foundation of therapy; applied immediately after bathing
  • Biologics: Dupilumab (Dupixent) for moderate-severe eczema
  • JAK inhibitors: Topical (crisaborole) and oral options for targeted immune control

Comprehensive Management Plan

  • Detailed skin examination and severity assessment
  • Patch testing if contact dermatitis suspected
  • Customized treatment plan with clear expectations
  • Monthly or quarterly follow-up assessments
  • Adjustment of therapies based on response
  • Lifestyle counseling (stress reduction, trigger avoidance, skincare routine)

Cost & Investment

Skin Cancer Screening & Treatment

Medical

Early detection and advanced treatment of melanoma, basal cell, and squamous cell carcinomas

Screening Services

  • Full-body skin examination: Head-to-toe assessment by board-certified dermatologist
  • Dermoscopy: Magnified examination with specialized dermatoscope for accurate diagnosis
  • Digital imaging: High-resolution photography for monitoring changes over time
  • Risk assessment: Personal and family history evaluation
  • Annual monitoring: Recommended for high-risk patients (history of melanoma, multiple nevi, fair skin)

Types of Skin Cancer

  • Basal Cell Carcinoma (BCC): Most common (80%); slow-growing, rarely metastasizes
  • Squamous Cell Carcinoma (SCC): Second most common (15%); more aggressive than BCC
  • Melanoma: Most dangerous (5%); can metastasize rapidly if not caught early
  • Merkel Cell Carcinoma: Rare but aggressive neuroendocrine cancer

Treatment Options

  • Mohs Micrographic Surgery: Gold standard for high-risk BCCs and SCCs; tissue-sparing, highest cure rate (98-99%)
  • Surgical excision: For lower-risk lesions; wide margins for complete removal
  • Cryotherapy: Liquid nitrogen freezing for early, small lesions
  • Topical medications: 5-FU or imiquimod for superficial lesions
  • Photodynamic therapy: Light-activated chemotherapy for pre-cancerous and early lesions
  • Systemic therapy: Targeted therapy and immunotherapy for advanced/metastatic disease

Prevention Counseling

  • Sunscreen: Daily SPF 30+ (SPF 50+ for high-risk patients)
  • Sun avoidance: Limit outdoor time 10 AM - 4 PM
  • Protective clothing: Long sleeves, hats, UV-blocking sunglasses
  • Avoid tanning beds: Increases melanoma risk by 75%
  • Regular self-checks: ABCDE rule for suspicious moles

Cost & Investment

Full-body screening: ₹8,000 - ₹15,000 | Mohs surgery (per stage): ₹30,000 - ₹60,000 | Excision surgery: ₹20,000 - ₹50,000 | Systemic therapy: ₹1,00,000+/month
